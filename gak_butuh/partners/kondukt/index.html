Kondukt

Intro: KonduktAI is revolutionizing the crypto and AI landscape by combining advanced AI with blockchain technology to manage decentralized physical infrastructure networks (DEPIN). We focus on the optimal use of GPUs, enabling significant enhancements in both bitcoin mining and powering decentralized LLMs, creating a dual-purpose utility that maximizes efficiency and profitability.

Our Product: KonduktAI is pioneering the tokenisation of GPU resources to create a scalable and flexible platform for both individuals and businesses to engage with cutting-edge AI and blockchain technology. We ensure optimal efficiency and profitability, guided by real-time AI-driven arbitrage decisions.
